About WoW Tokens

WoW Tokens can be purchased for real money and sold on the in-game auction house for gold. Alternatively, players can purchase tokens with gold and redeem them for Battle.net Balance or game time.

Prices fluctuate based on supply and demand. Data is updated from Blizzard's API in real-time.
